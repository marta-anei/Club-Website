My Grandmother had everything

'My Grandmother had everything:
She had a porch with Geraniums,
She had a fountain, bread, and salt,
She had the strength, patience and all.
She had faith and she had love,
And a great longing for salvation,
She had a horse tied to a cart,
And the good cow's beloved white potion.
She had countless stories of the life,
And unsung songs of bravest hearts,
Which she often told out slowly,
At corvees and evening sittings.'
